Instructions

  1. Open up the following link: https://upbeat-fermat-0a0503.netlify.com/
  2. Follow the link to your city
  3. Resize your browser window to the size you'd like your email to be (for this, I found that on my 15" Mac the email looked best at 2/3s of my screen)
  4. Selected all with "Command + a"
  5. Copy all with "Command + c"
  6. Open up gmail.com
  7. Compose a new email
  8. [OPTIONAL] Remove any automatic email signature you may have
  9. Paste with "Command + p" into the body of the email
  10. Edit the text at "Hi {{name}}" and the date/location data for your city's party
  11. Do a test run by sending it to yourself to ensure you resized the browser to good size in step 3
  12. Repeat steps 3-10 and send to the employees' attending the city's party
